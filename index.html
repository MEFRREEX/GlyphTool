How to Use

1. Upload a glyph image (e.g., glyph_E1.png) from your Minecraft resource pack.

2. The image will be divided into a 16√ó16 grid of glyphs.

3. Click on any glyph to copy its Unicode character to clipboard.

4. Use the copied Unicode in your Minecraft text components.

Format Explained

The Unicode format is \uE1A2 where:

- E1 comes from the glyph filename (glyph_E1.png)

- A2 is the grid position (A = column 10, 2 = row 2)

First square is 00, then 01...0F, then 10...FF.
